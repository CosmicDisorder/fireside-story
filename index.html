It was a dark and stormy night...

The city was silent save for the keening hum of the frigid winter wind.

This scene remained still, save the occasional blurp of a wayward keystroke...


. . . Keystroke? "Oh no!" Jack cried, as he leapt up from his computer, rubbing at the imprint his keyboard had left on his cheek. "I overslept---and today's the day of the meeting!"

Jack's sudden movement startles his cat, Gary, who also sleeping was nestled up on the mousepads.

"Whoops, sorry, Felix!" he said, as he threw on his jacket, grabbed his laptop, and flew out the door.
